What is Batik?
We choose batik as the theme of our project. The word batik is derived from the Javanese word “ambatik” in origin, where amba is a verb. It means"to write" and titik is a noun that means "dot".

Batik is the technically and artistically demanding tradition of wax-resist dyeing (aka Laweyan ) . And it also refers to the cloth made using this technique.
